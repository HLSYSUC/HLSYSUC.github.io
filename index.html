About

Hi, my name is Hanlin. I am a faculty member at the University of Connecticut. I took my PhD at the University of Toronto. My research focuses on how the built environment is associated with health behaviors, crime risks, and urban resilience. If you are interested in any of these topics, I am happy to collaborate.
